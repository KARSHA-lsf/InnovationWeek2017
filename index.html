Crowdsourcing

Crowdsourcing is the process of getting work or funding, usually online, from a crowd of people. The idea is to take work and outsource it to a crowd of workers. This app takes users GPS coordinates to track trains. It does not use anything else. Therefore, app users is the crowd this app uses to gather information.

Live Tracking

Real-time locating systems (RTLS) are used to automatically identify and track the location of objects or people in real time. You just need a smartphone with GPS capability. This allows the train location to be displayed against a map backdrop in real time by tracking app users.

Smart Idea

Since this app does not use any device to track trains, this concept can also be used to track any other vehicles as well. It certainly much cheaper than tracking using "GPS Tracking Unit". There isn't much rules and regulations that affect to this legally. Only thing we need is users permission to get his/her GPS coordinates when installing this application.

Problem

Trains are perhaps the most reliable form of transport. They are not only cheap but are also very convenient for long distances. Despite the fact that trains are relatively new to Kashmir, they have become the most used form of transport. Everyday hundreds of commuters board these metallic boxes to reach their various destinations. Some people even consider them to be a blessing given the increasing frequency in the number of traffic jams.

However, this efficient system of transportation too has some disadvantages. Given the heavy rush of passengers and in absence of any crowd management, overcrowding in the trains has become a nightmare for the daily commuters. Lack of passenger facilities, like washrooms, enough ticket counters etc. are also causing inconveniences to the public.

Here we addressed one of the major issues, train delays. So far, the only way to know about train delays is, going to the railway station and listening to the announcements going on there. Instead, using this app, anyone can find where their train is from anywhere. If it seems to be a train delay, then user can make a decision either to wait for the train or to use another transport method.

Big Benefits for train passengers...

  • Find your train from anytime anywhere...
  • Get notifications about train delays...
  • Share your information with others via chat function...

Concept

MobileTrain is a mobile application which tracks trains using app users GPS locations data. Firebase databse already has a table which contains GPS coordinates of railway tracks. When the user opens the app, his/her GPS coordinates are sent to the database for every 30 seconds. By analysing those data it finds a pattern of how those coordinates vary with the time. If users GPS coordinates variation matches with railway tracks coordinates, then it decides those users are on a train and it displays that location as the current location of that train.

This application does not use any device to track trains. It only uses GPS coordinates from app users. Therefore, passengers can find where the trains is, without going to the railway station itself. If someone has registered in the system, they will be notified about train delays in advanced. Chat functionality helps users to share their experiences with others.
